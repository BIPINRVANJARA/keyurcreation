About Keyurscreation

Capturing timeless wedding stories through emotion, artistry, and cinematic excellence.

0+

Years of
Experience

Keyurscreation – Stories by Keyur | TSPF is a passionate wedding photography and cinematography studio based in Bhiloda, Gujarat. We specialize in capturing the raw emotions, candid laughter, and sacred rituals that make Indian weddings unforgettable.

With a storytelling approach, premium editing, and cinematic filmmaking, we serve couples across Bhiloda, Himatnagar, Gandhinagar, Ahmedabad, and all of Gujarat — turning every celebration into a timeless visual legacy.
